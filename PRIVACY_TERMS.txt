━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. WHAT WE COLLECT
   Avryd collects:
   - Your email address and OAuth identity (for authentication)
   - A hardware fingerprint of your device (for license binding)
   - Usage session duration (for your dashboard statistics)
   - Crash logs if you enable error reporting

2. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT
   - Screen content, text read by the screen reader
   - Passwords or sensitive form data
   - Keystrokes or typed text
   - Your browsing history or app usage

3. DATA STORAGE
   Your authentication data is stored securely on Supabase.
   All local settings are stored on your device in
   %LOCALAPPDATA%\Avryd and never transmitted unless you
   explicitly use a cloud-sync feature.

4. THIRD PARTIES
   We use Supabase for authentication storage.
   OAuth sign-in uses Google, Microsoft, or Facebook.
   We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

5. YOUR RIGHTS
   You may delete your account at any time from the dashboard.
   Account deletion removes all server-side data within 30 days.
